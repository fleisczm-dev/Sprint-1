About Us!

Our team is composed of 5 students analyzing the effects/struggles of AI in the workplace.
AI can automate many facets of the workplace, automating systems that used to take human production and creativity to produce. The landscape of AI is advancing at a rapid rate as well, so we are in the context of what can be done today, it is subject to be expanded upon in the near future. While this is significant, there is debate on the implications of substituting human work for an automated system. However, if we look at things from a business perspective, AI usage could rapidly cut costs and make the workplace more efficient for the owner, as well as the employees. We are outlining in the following pages the ability to use AI for the practices of business.
